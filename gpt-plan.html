Neck work — every training day
MMA is brutal on necks and yours has been inactive for a year. Do these before every session as part of warm-up, or during your calf stretch breaks. 3×10 sec isometric holds in 4 directions: hand on forehead push forward, hand behind head push back, hand on each temple push side. Plus 10 slow neck circles each direction. Takes 3 minutes. Never skip.
🦵
Calf return protocol
Jogging only begins when you pass this test: walk 45 min at brisk pace pain-free, then do 15 single-leg calf raises on the affected leg, and have zero soreness the next morning. Only then: start with 1 min jog / 2 min walk intervals. Jump rope stays banned for the first 4 weeks of running return.
🥗
Recovery baseline
Protein: 1.6–2g per kg bodyweight daily (~100–140g for most). Sleep: 7–9 hrs — this is where adaptation happens. Hydration: ~35ml per kg bodyweight, more on training days. Carbs: don't cut them on training days — you need fuel for the nervous system. Deconditioned + underfed = injury.
